# Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer churn behavior and identify the key factors contributing to customer attrition. It provides actionable insights into customer demographics, service usage, payment methods, contracts, and churn categories to help businesses improve customer retention and reduce churn.

## Features

**Overview Dashboard:**

Provides a high-level summary of key business metrics, including Total Customers, New Joiners, Total Churn, and Overall Churn Rate with interactive KPI cards.

**Churn Rate Analysis:**

Analyzes churn rates by state, internet type, payment method, contract type, and customer tenure to identify high-risk customer segments.

**Customer Demographics:**

Examines customer churn by gender and age groups, helping businesses understand demographic patterns and customer behavior.

**Detailed Insights:**

Provides in-depth analysis of churn by state, internet type, churn category, and customer tenure to uncover the primary drivers of customer attrition.

**Interactive Dashboard:**

Includes dynamic slicers for State, Payment Method, Contract, Internet Type, and Churn Category, allowing users to explore data from multiple perspectives.

**Insightful Visualizations:**

Utilizes KPI cards, bar charts, line charts, donut charts, and combination charts to deliver clear, interactive, and actionable business insights for customer retention strategies.
